Our Story

Proudly sharing the beauty of Afghan culture through clothing since 1990.

Honoring Our Roots

Our founder Ali started this company in 1990 to share the rich cultural heritage of Afghanistan through traditional clothing. After moving to the US, Ali missed the vibrant colors and luxurious fabrics of his homeland. He began importing high-quality Afghan textiles and clothing to offer a touch of home to fellow immigrants.

Over the years, we've grown from a small startup to an internationally recognized Afghan fashion brand. But our mission remains the same - honoring the artistry of Afghanistan's weavers, tailors, and designers to bring you authentic cultural treasures.

  • Founded in 1990

  • Expanded internationally in early 2000s

About Khaista Style

Khaista Style sells traditional Afghani dresses, coats, and accessories for women. Our mission is to preserve Afghan heritage and empower local artisans by providing an international platform for their work.
