Meet the Talented Craftspeople Behind Our Clothes

Fatima

Fatima is a master embroiderer from Herat who leads a cooperative of 15 skilled artisans. Her team hand-stitches the beautiful embroidery on our dresses and accessories.

Zuhal

Zuhal weaves high-quality wool for our coats and jackets in her small family-run workshop. Her dedication to traditional techniques is evident in their unmatched warmth and softness.

Zarmeena

A master tailor for over 20 years, Zarmeena leads the sewing team that carefully crafts each piece. She hand-selects fine silks and cottons for the dresses she designs herself.

About Khaista Style

Khaista Style sells traditional Afghani dresses, coats, and accessories for women. Our mission is to preserve Afghan heritage and empower local artisans by providing an international platform for their work.
